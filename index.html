N1 は N2です。

  1. Particle は

    This particle indicates that the word before it is the topic of the sentence. You select a noun you want to talk about, add は to show that it is the topic and give a statement about the topic. Note: The particle は is read わ

    1. わたしはラシェドル•ハイダーです。　I am Rashedul Haydar.
  2. です

    Nouns used with です work as predicates.
    です indicates judgement or assertion.
    です also conveys that the speaker is being polite towards the listener.
    です inflects when the sentence is negative or in the past tense.

    1. わたしはエンジニアです。　I am an engineer.
